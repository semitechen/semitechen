## Hi, I'm Kacper

I am an embedded systems developer studying Microelectronics in Industry and Medicine at AGH University of Krakow. My work spans the entire hardware creation process—from designing PCBs and writing C firmware, to leveraging my past web development experience with TypeScript and React to build the supporting software.

### What I am working on
* **MTT16:** My flagship project. It is a hardware sequencer dedicated to DJs for creating and performing music live. It is currently in active development, built using KiCad, and financed by the [Integra Science Club](https://integra.agh.edu.pl/).
* **Hardware Design:** Currently designing PCBs using Altium Designer for my university coursework and Hardwire projects, alongside my primary use of KiCad for independent builds like MTT16.
* **Architecture:** Working hands-on with ARM Cortex-M and RISC-V microcontrollers.

### Community & Leadership
* **Founder and Leader** of the [Hardwire Science Club](https://github.com/KN-Hardwire/).
* **Project Coordinator** at the [Integra Science Club](https://integra.agh.edu.pl/).
